Welcome to Gina Hairstylist, Inc.

Serving you since 1980.

We are a family owned, independent local hair salon in the heart of Brooklyn. With over 30 years of experience, our reputation sets us apart. We pride ourselves in offering professional hair services that inspire and empower our customers. We create styles that are stunning, unique and true to your personality and look.
Our team is looking forward to meeting you!

The Team

Our team is comitted to providing total care and satisfaction while creating unique styles that underline your natural beauty. With a combined experience of over 80 years, you can rest assured that your hair is in expert hands. Each of our stylists work with their clients to develop their unique and authentic personal style.

"Gina" Maria

Senior Hairstylist

Being a hair stylist for over forty years, Maria has dedicated her life to perfecting the art of styling hair and embolden her loyal customers. Part founder and also a Master Hairstylist, she has trained with many masters known throughout the industry, such as Tony & Guy, and Vidal Sassoon. Her expertise in short haircuts is one of Brooklyn's best kept secrets.

Founder

Clara

Master Stylist

Clara began following her passion for hair at the age of seventeen.
